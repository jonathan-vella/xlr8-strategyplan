# Workshop Overview: Mastering the CAF Strategy and Planning Phase

## About This Workshop
This workshop is designed to help participants develop a comprehensive cloud adoption strategy and plan for StoreConnect Retail, a growing regional retailer. Using the Microsoft Cloud Adoption Framework (CAF), participants will address StoreConnect's business challenges, define strategic goals, and create actionable plans for Azure adoption.

## Target Audience
This workshop is intended for:
- IT decision-makers and strategists
- Cloud architects and consultants
- Business leaders involved in digital transformation
- Technical teams supporting cloud adoption initiatives
- Finance teams involved with cloud spending and governance

## Duration
The workshop is structured into five challenges, with a total duration of approximately 7 hours and 15 minutes:
- **Challenge 1**: Define the Cloud Adoption Strategy (1hr 45mins)
- **Challenge 2**: FinOps Review & Assessment (1hr 15mins)
- **Challenge 3**: Inform Strategy & Address Risks (1hr 15mins)
- **Challenge 4**: Develop the Cloud Adoption Plan (1hr 30mins)
- **Challenge 5**: Present the Strategy & Plan to Leadership (1hr 30mins)

## Pre-requisites
To get the most out of this workshop, participants should have:
- A basic understanding of cloud computing concepts
- Familiarity with Microsoft Azure services and capabilities
- Knowledge of their organization's IT environment and business goals
- **Azure Fundamentals certification** or equivalent knowledge is highly recommended

## Key Takeaways
By the end of this workshop, participants will:
- Understand how to apply the Microsoft Cloud Adoption Framework to real-world scenarios
- Develop a cloud adoption strategy aligned with business goals
- Apply FinOps principles to establish cloud financial governance
- Identify and mitigate risks associated with cloud adoption
- Create a high-level cloud adoption plan with prioritized workloads
- Present a compelling business case for Azure adoption to executive leadership
