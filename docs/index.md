# XLR8: Mastering the CAF Strategy and Planning Phase

![StoreConnect Retail](../media/images/storeconnect-retail.png)

## Workshop Overview

This workshop is designed to help participants develop a comprehensive cloud adoption strategy and plan for StoreConnect Retail, a growing regional retailer. Using the Microsoft Cloud Adoption Framework (CAF), participants will address StoreConnect's business challenges, define strategic goals, create actionable plans for Azure adoption, and implement FinOps practices for cloud cost management.

## Workshop Structure

The workshop is structured into five challenges, with a total duration of approximately 7 hours and 15 minutes:

- [Challenge 1: Define the Cloud Adoption Strategy](./challenge1) (1hr 45mins)
- [Challenge 2: FinOps Review & Assessment](./challenge2) (1hr 15mins)
- [Challenge 3: Inform Strategy & Address Risks](./challenge3) (1hr 15mins)
- [Challenge 4: Develop the Cloud Adoption Plan](./challenge4) (1hr 30mins)
- [Challenge 5: Present the Strategy & Plan to Leadership](./challenge5) (1hr 30mins)

## Target Audience

This workshop is intended for:
- IT decision-makers and strategists
- Cloud architects and consultants
- Business leaders involved in digital transformation
- Technical teams supporting cloud adoption initiatives
- Finance teams involved with cloud spending and governance

## Pre-requisites

To get the most out of this workshop, participants should have:
- A basic understanding of cloud computing concepts
- Familiarity with Microsoft Azure services and capabilities
- Knowledge of their organization's IT environment and business goals
- **Azure Fundamentals certification** or equivalent knowledge is highly recommended
