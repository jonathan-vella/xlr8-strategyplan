# XLR8: Mastering the CAF Strategy and Planning Phase

## Challenge 3 - Inform Strategy & Address Risks (1hr 15mins)

In this challenge, you'll enhance StoreConnect's cloud adoption strategy by addressing key strategic considerations and business risks. You'll use the "Inform Strategy" phase of the Cloud Adoption Framework to ensure StoreConnect's strategy accounts for critical areas like cost efficiency, security, AI integration, and resiliency, and develop a risk management approach.

**Learning Objectives:**
- Apply CAF strategic considerations to enhance the cloud adoption strategy
- Identify key business risks associated with cloud adoption
- Develop mitigation strategies leveraging Azure capabilities
- Begin organizational readiness planning

---

## Key Tasks

### Task 1: Address Key Strategic Considerations
For each of the following strategic areas, develop specific recommendations for StoreConnect that inform their cloud strategy:

- **Cost Efficiency**
  - Analyze StoreConnect's current IT spend distribution
  - Identify opportunities for cost optimization in Azure
  - Recommend approaches to manage cloud spending (e.g., Azure Cost Management, reservations for predictable workloads)

- **AI Integration**
  - Identify specific retail processes that could benefit from AI
  - Outline how Azure AI services could address StoreConnect's challenges
  - Recommend an approach for responsible AI implementation

- **Security and Compliance**
  - Identify retail-specific security and compliance requirements
  - Recommend Azure security capabilities to address these needs
  - Outline a security-first approach to cloud adoption

- **Resiliency**
  - Assess critical workloads requiring high availability
  - Recommend Azure resiliency patterns for retail workloads
  - Define an approach to business continuity in the cloud

### Task 2: Identify and Address Business Risks
- Identify 5-7 key business risks StoreConnect faces with cloud adoption
- Categorize risks by impact (high/medium/low) and likelihood
- For each risk, identify specific Azure capabilities that help mitigate the risk
- Develop a concise risk management approach that would address executive concerns

### Task 3: Prepare for Organizational Alignment
- Identify potential organizational barriers to cloud adoption at StoreConnect
- Recommend approaches to secure executive buy-in and sponsorship
- Assess whether a project or product delivery model is appropriate
- Outline initial steps for organizational readiness

---

## Challenge 3 – Success Criteria

1. Develop specific recommendations for each strategic consideration area (cost, AI, security, resiliency)
2. Create a prioritized risk register with 5-7 risks, their potential impacts, and mitigation approaches
3. Map Azure services and capabilities to each identified risk
4. Present an organizational readiness assessment with recommendations for addressing gaps
