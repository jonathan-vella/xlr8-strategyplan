# XLR8: Mastering the CAF Strategy and Planning Phase

## Challenge 1 - Define the Cloud Adoption Strategy (1hr 45mins)

In this challenge, you will establish StoreConnect Retail's cloud adoption strategy following the Cloud Adoption Framework's strategy methodology. You'll assess their readiness, define motivations, establish business outcomes, build an initial business justification, and identify the strategy team.

**Learning Objectives:**
- Apply CAF Strategy methodology to develop a cohesive cloud adoption strategy
- Identify and document business motivations and outcomes
- Create a preliminary business case for Azure adoption
- Define the cloud strategy team structure

---

## Key Tasks

### Task 1: Assess StoreConnect's Cloud Adoption Strategy
- Use the [Cloud Adoption Strategy Evaluator](https://learn.microsoft.com/en-us/assessments/8fefc6d5-97ac-42b3-8e97-d82701e55bab/) to evaluate StoreConnect's current state and identify key gaps
- Document 3-4 critical areas where StoreConnect's cloud strategy needs development
- Identify the biggest strategic challenges that might hinder successful cloud adoption

### Task 2: Define StoreConnect's Motivations for Cloud Adoption 
- Based on the customer scenario, identify and classify primary motivations for cloud adoption using the CAF motivations framework:
  - **Critical business events** (datacenter refresh cycles, competitive pressures)
  - **Migration motivations** (cost savings, operational efficiencies)
  - **Innovation motivations** (customer experiences, data insights, AI opportunities)
- Create a prioritized list of these motivations with justifications

### Task 3: Define Mission and Business Outcomes
- Create a cloud mission statement that aligns with StoreConnect's business strategy
- Define 4-6 specific, measurable business outcomes that address the identified motivations
- Establish key performance indicators (KPIs) for each business outcome
- Map how Azure capabilities will help achieve these outcomes

### Task 4: Build Initial Business Justification
- Develop a high-level business justification for cloud adoption using the available financial information
- Include estimated costs, benefits, and ROI timeline for the migration
- Identify key assumptions made in your calculations
- Consider both migration and innovation aspects in your justification

### Task 5: Define the Cloud Strategy Team
- Identify key stakeholders and roles needed for StoreConnect's cloud strategy team
- Define responsibilities and interactions between these roles
- Consider both business and technical representation
- Address any organizational silos that could impact cloud adoption

---

## Challenge 1 – Success Criteria

1. Complete and document the Cloud Adoption Strategy Evaluator assessment with screenshot(s)
2. Present a prioritized list of at least 5 motivations for StoreConnect's cloud adoption
3. Define 4-6 specific business outcomes with corresponding KPIs
4. Create a high-level business justification showing projected ROI timeline
5. Outline the recommended cloud strategy team structure with key roles and responsibilities
